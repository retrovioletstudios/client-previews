Common Questions
What To Expect
Do I need an appointment before bringing my car in? +
While we do accept walk-ins for true emergencies, our lot is frequently fully booked out. We highly recommend using our digital request form or calling ahead to secure a spot in our queue so you aren't left waiting.
Will you provide an estimate before doing any work? +
Absolutely. We operate on 100% transparency. Once we diagnose the issue, we will call you to explain exactly what is wrong and how much it will cost. We never turn a wrench or charge you for a repair until you explicitly approve the quote.
Can I drop my vehicle off after hours? +
Yes! We have a secure key-drop box located near the front office. Simply park your car in an available space, fill out the drop-off envelope with your contact information, put your keys inside, and drop it in the slot. We'll call you first thing the next morning.
