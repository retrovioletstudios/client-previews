For an impressive thirty-two years, D&H Auto has served the communities of Round Rock, Hutto, and the greater Austin area. We are not just a mechanic shop—we are a premier auto body and collision repair center dedicated to highest quality comprehensive automotive repair.

We understand the immense stress that follows a vehicular collision. Our goal is to alleviate that burden entirely. We act as your direct insurance ally, providing comprehensive acceptance of all auto insurances, seamless bureaucratic assistance with claims, and critical deductible support.

From the moment you upload your damage photos to the moment you drive your flawlessly restored, showroom-ready vehicle off our lot, we guarantee an unmatched level of quality and service.
